Infrastructure

World-Class School Facilities

Segero provides state-of-the-art equipment and infrastructure that enhance both teaching and learning for every student.

🔬
Biology Laboratory
Fully equipped lab for dissection, microscopy, and biological experiments aligned to the KCSE syllabus.
⚗️
Chemistry Laboratory
Well-stocked chemistry lab with reagents, glassware, and safety equipment for hands-on practical learning.
🧲
Physics Laboratory
Equipped with instruments for mechanics, electricity, optics, and all practical components of the KCSE Physics syllabus.
💻
Computer Laboratory
Modern computer lab with sufficient machines to prepare students for ICT-driven careers and the digital world.
🏠
Home Science Room
A fully resourced Home Science facility with equipment for cookery, textile work, and child development practicals.
🎵
Music Room
Students can develop musical talent on piano, guitar, drums, flute, and other instruments in a dedicated music room.
🏠
Modern Dormitories
Spacious, comfortable boarding facilities for boys and girls, providing a safe and nurturing home away from home.
💧
Borehole Water Supply
The school has its own dedicated borehole ensuring clean, reliable water supply throughout the year for all students and staff.
⚡
24-Hour Power Supply
Steady KPLC electricity supply backed by an automatic standby generator ensuring uninterrupted power at all times.
