Our Story

Who We Are

Segero Adventist High School is a private, mixed boarding school situated in Uasin Gishu County, Eldoret West Sub-County, approximately 50 kilometres north-west of Eldoret town along the Eldoret–Ziwa–Kitale Road.

The school is a religious, non-profit institution fully affiliated with the Seventh-day Adventist (SDA) Church, operating under the Greater Rift Valley Conference as part of the East-Central Africa Division of the Adventist world church.

As part of the Segero Group of Schools (SGS), we operate under centralised management alongside Segero National Academy, other Segero high schools, and Segero Academy primary school — offering every learner a home within the Segero family.

Our Journey

School History & Milestones

From humble beginnings to a nationally recognised institution, Segero's story is one of faith, perseverance, and growth.

SGS

Founding of Segero Group of Schools

Segero was established as a Seventh-day Adventist educational initiative in the Rift Valley region of Kenya, providing quality, faith-based secondary education to students from across the country.

↑

Expansion Under SGS

Due to growing admission demands, the school expanded into a centralised group — Segero Group of Schools (SGS) — comprising multiple schools at different entry thresholds to accommodate more learners while maintaining high standards.

2018

KCSE 2018 — B+ Mean Score

Segero Adventist High School registered 182 KCSE candidates and achieved a mean grade of B+ with a performance index of 71.048 — one of the school's strongest results, affirming its place among Kenya's top private schools.

2019

Top 200 Schools in Kenya

The school was featured in the national list of top 200 secondary schools in Kenya following the 2019 KCSE results — a proud achievement that reflects sustained academic excellence.

2023

100% C+ and Above — KCSE 2023

All registered candidates in the 2023 KCSE examinations attained a minimum of C+, a milestone that drew widespread recognition and celebration from the school community.

Now

Junior Secondary School Launched

Segero has expanded further by introducing a Junior Secondary School (JSS) for Grades 6, 7 and 8, extending the school's holistic, faith-based education to younger learners within the same campus and community.

Our Church Connection

Seventh-day Adventist Education

Segero Adventist High School is part of the worldwide SDA education system — one of the largest Protestant educational networks in the world, operating thousands of schools across more than 140 countries.

Our affiliation with the Greater Rift Valley Conference means our students benefit from a curriculum that integrates academic rigour with strong Christian values, preparing them for service, leadership, and eternal life.
