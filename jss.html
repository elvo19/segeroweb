Our Junior School

A Strong Foundation for Future Excellence

Segero Adventist High School has expanded its mission by establishing a Junior Secondary School (JSS) catering to learners in Grades 6, 7, and 8 under Kenya's Competency Based Curriculum (CBC).

The JSS is fully integrated within the Segero campus, meaning junior learners benefit from the same excellent facilities, dedicated staff, and strong SDA values that have made Segero a trusted name in Kenyan education for decades.

Our junior secondary students transition seamlessly into Form One of the senior high school, building on a foundation of academic confidence, spiritual grounding, and strong character.

Three Year Programme

Grades 6, 7 & 8

Our Junior Secondary programme takes learners through three progressive years of competency-based education in a structured, supportive boarding environment.

6
Grade 6
The entry year into Junior Secondary. Students build strong foundations in all learning areas while adapting to boarding life and the structured routines of the Segero community. Emphasis on literacy, numeracy, and social skills.
7
Grade 7
In the middle year, learners deepen their competencies across all subjects. Co-curricular activities, clubs, and spiritual programmes become a significant part of school life, developing the whole person.
8
Grade 8
The final year of Junior Secondary prepares students for the Kenya Junior School Education Assessment (KJSEA) and the transition to Form One. Academic focus intensifies as students are equipped for senior secondary success.
